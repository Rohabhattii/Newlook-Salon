Our Story

Beauty Refined Through Experience

✦

At New Look Salon, beauty is an art form — one we have refined through experience, care, and an unwavering attention to detail. Led by Quratulain Imran, with over 18 years of deep expertise in the beauty industry, our salon is built on trust, skill, and a genuine understanding of what truly suits you.

Every client walks into a warm, modern space and receives treatment that is thoughtfully tailored — never rushed, never generic. From nourishing facials and spa therapies to precision hair transformations, we bring everything under one roof.

Our specialties span keratin, nanoplastia, hair botox, balayage, and bespoke coloring — each performed with the confidence that only two decades of practice can offer. Whether you seek an understated everyday look or a full bridal transformation, we ensure a flawless finish every single time.
